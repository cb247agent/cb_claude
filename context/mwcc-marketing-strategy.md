# MWCC Marketing Strategy

**Read this when generating campaign briefs, content strategy, ad targeting, or KPI frameworks for My World Childcare.**

For full brand context, see `context/mwcc-brand-context.md`. For competitor specifics, see `context/mwcc-competitors.md`.

---

## Business goals (group-level)

1. **Maintain network occupancy at 70%+** across the 5 centres
2. **Net enrolments ≥ 0** every week — enrolments must equal or beat exits
3. **Cost per enrolment under $250** — paid acquisition must be financially viable
4. **GBP visibility on the local pack** for "childcare [suburb]" queries for all 5 centres
5. **Compliance status: zero rooms over capacity** — regulatory risk is unacceptable

## What success looks like by Q4 2026

- Average network occupancy: 75% (currently ~51%)
- Net enrolments: +20/quarter consistently
- Cost per enrolment: <$200
- All 5 centres on the GBP local pack for their suburb's main query
- Performance Review hit rate: 60%+ (winner verdicts on actions)

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)

### ICP 1 — Working parents needing LDC (primary)
- **Who:** Dual-income households, 25-40, kids 0-5
- **Pain:** Childcare drop-off vs work hours · CCS confusion · waitlist anxiety
- **Where:** Within 6km of a centre
- **Conversion path:** GBP local pack → centre landing page → tour booking
- **Centres relevant:** Midvale · Seville Grove · Waikiki (LDC services)

### ICP 2 — School-age parents needing OSHC (secondary)
- **Who:** Working parents with primary-school-age kids (5-12)
- **Pain:** Before/after school care gaps · school holiday coverage · pickup logistics
- **Where:** Catchment for nearby primary schools
- **Conversion path:** Local school newsletter → OSHC enrolment form
- **Centres relevant:** Armadale · Midvale · Rockingham · Seville Grove

### ICP 3 — CCS-aware budget-conscious families
- **Who:** Fee-conscious parents — CCS makes childcare viable for them
- **Pain:** Comparing total weekly cost (gross fee minus CCS) across providers
- **Where:** Researching online — KindiCare, Care for Kids comparison sites
- **Conversion path:** Comparison search → MWCC quote calculator → tour
- **Centres relevant:** All 5

### ICP 4 — Vacation Care families (seasonal)
- **Who:** Parents needing school holiday coverage 4× year
- **Pain:** Last-minute holiday programmes filling up
- **Where:** Catchment of OSHC centres
- **Conversion path:** Term-end email → Vacation Care booking
- **Centres relevant:** Armadale · Midvale · Rockingham · Seville Grove

## Seasonal calendar (Perth childcare)

| Period | Demand signal | Marketing focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Jan–Feb** | New year enrolment rush — many families finalising kindy + LDC decisions | Tours · GBP photos · landing page A/B tests |
| **March–April** | Term 1 settle-in; OSHC waitlists fill | Vacation Care promo for April school holidays |
| **May–June** | Mid-year enquiry slowdown | Content marketing · long-form blog publishing |
| **July** | Mid-year intake bump for term 3 | Tour push · open-day events |
| **Aug–Sept** | Q4 planning conversations begin | Kindy 2027 waitlist · pre-school readiness content |
| **Oct–Nov** | Christmas + Jan 2027 enrolment decisions | Heavy paid promotion · enrolment forms · waitlist |

WA-specific events:
- WA Day (June 4 long weekend) — closure communications
- WA school terms — different to other states; verify dates each year
- Term holidays drive Vacation Care demand spikes

See `context/mwcc-competitors.md` for full competitor analysis. Key differentiators:

|---|---|

| CCS-savvy fee guidance | Aggregators (KindiCare, Care for Kids) only show listings, not personal guidance |

## What NOT to claim

Australian Consumer Law + NQS regulations require claims to be defensible. Don't write:
- "Best childcare in Perth" / "#1 childcare" — undefendable
- "Award-winning" unless we have a named, current award
- "Specialised therapy services" unless we actually have qualified specialists
- "Guaranteed" anything — outcomes can't be guaranteed
- NQS rating numbers we don't currently hold
