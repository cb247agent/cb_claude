## Battle Cards

### vs Revo Fitness (PRIORITY THREAT)
- Revo wins on: price at entry level ($9.69), brand awareness in Perth
- CB247 wins on: Kids Hub, Sauna + Ice Bath, Neon21/ChasingRX classes, community
- Key message: "Same price, but we have everything they don't"

### vs Ryderwear Gym Malaga (LOCATION THREAT)
- Ryderwear wins on: premium gym aesthetic, serious lifters crowd, apparel brand recognition
- CB247 wins on: price, group fitness variety, Reformer Pilates, Kids Hub, FIFO freeze
- Key message: "Train hard AND bring the family"

### vs Anytime/Snap (AWARENESS THREAT)
- They win on: national brand recognition
- CB247 wins on: price, facilities, local Perth community
- Key message: "Local gym, premium facilities, half the price"
