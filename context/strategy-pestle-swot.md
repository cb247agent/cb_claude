# Strategy: PESTLE + SWOT — ChasingBetter247

## PESTLE Analysis (External)

POLITICAL
- WA council regulations for 24/7 unstaffed gym access
- FIFO industry workforce policy — supports flexible membership needs
- Health & safety compliance for recovery facilities (sauna/ice bath)

ECONOMIC
- Perth cost-of-living pressure → $11.95/week is highly competitive
- WA mining boom = FIFO workers with high disposable income
- Post-COVID fitness spend recovering strongly in WA

SOCIAL
- Reformer Pilates & recovery trending nationally
- Family gym culture growing — Kids Hub demand up
- FIFO workers need flexible, no-fuss gym access
- Strong Perth local-first mentality (prefer local over national chains)

TECHNOLOGICAL
- Wearables (Apple Watch/Garmin) increasing member engagement expectations
- Meta Reels & short-form video dominant for gym marketing
- AI content automation reducing cost of quality marketing
- Booking/timetable apps now baseline expectation

LEGAL
- 24/7 unstaffed liability — duty of care requirements
- Member data privacy (Australian Privacy Act)
- WA health & safety for sauna/ice bath facilities

ENVIRONMENTAL
- 24/7 operation = high electricity cost → solar opportunity + brand story
- Perth climate → outdoor training content opportunity

## SWOT Analysis (Internal)

STRENGTHS
- $11.95/week — most competitive price vs premium facility ratio in Perth
- 8,000+ members — strong community base
- Only gym with Kids Hub + Sauna + Ice Bath + Reformer Pilates + 24/7
- FIFO freeze — unique WA positioning, no direct competitor
- Neon21 & ChasingRX — proprietary class brands, not available elsewhere

WEAKNESSES
- Marketing currently manual — no automation running yet
- 2 locations only vs national competitor scale
- Brand awareness lower than Anytime/Snap/Revo in Perth
- Transitioning from paid tools (Klaviyo/HubSpot) — temporary gap

OPPORTUNITIES
- AI automation = agency-quality marketing at near-zero cost
- Reformer Pilates & recovery culture trending → hero content opportunity
- FIFO market in northern Perth entirely un-owned by competitors
- Kids Hub = strong family market differentiation in Ellenbrook
- Local Perth brand story resonates vs national chain feeling

THREATS
- Revo Fitness — Perth-native, similar price, expanding fast
- Ryderwear Malaga — direct location overlap, premium positioning
- Recession risk — gym memberships cut first
- Revo adding Reformer Pilates to more Perth locations

---

## Strategic Priorities (from PESTLE + SWOT)
1. OWN the FIFO segment — no competitor can claim this
2. WIN on value story — premium facilities, lowest price
3. BUILD AI automation fast — biggest efficiency gap vs competitors
4. LEVERAGE Kids Hub + Recovery — unique combo no Perth gym has
