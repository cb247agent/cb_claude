## WA-SPECIFIC NOTES

- **WA school term dates differ from other Australian states** — always verify against official Department of Education WA calendar each year.
- **WA Day = first Monday of June** — all centres closed.
- **Queen's Birthday WA = end of September** (different from Eastern states) — falls inside Term 3 holidays in 2026.
- **Vacation Care runs DURING school holidays but NOT on public holidays** — communicate VC closure days to booked families ≥2 weeks ahead.
- **Reconciliation Week (27 May - 3 Jun) + NAIDOC Week (typically first week July) + Indigenous Literacy Day (Sept)** — cultural content opportunities. Coordinate with educators for child-led activities. Consent-required if any child involvement.
