# Psychological Triggers Framework — ChasingBetter247
Related: [[00-Index]] | [[Brand-Voice]] | [[Campaign-History]]

## Core Framework
Every piece of content must include at least 2 of these triggers to maximize conversion:

### 1. IDENTITY
Frame CB247 as who they already are, not who they need to become.
- **Formula:** "People like you train here"
- **Application:** Connect the gym experience to the user's existing self-image (e.g., "The strongest mum in the room is already you").

### 2. SOCIAL PROOF
Always include numbers, names, and specific suburb references to build local trust.
- **Formula:** "[X] Perth locals + specific suburb reference"
- **Application:** "8,000+ Perth members from Malaga and Ellenbrook."

### 3. LOSS AVERSION
Lead with what they lose by NOT joining.
- **Formula:** "Every week you wait = [specific cost/loss]"
- **Application:** Mentioning that $0 joining fee offers are time-limited and once gone, the cost increases.

### 4. SAFETY
Remove the #1 objection before they think it.
- **Formula:** "No lock-in. No judgment. No risk."
- **Application:** Explicitly stating the lack of contracts and the welcoming atmosphere.

### 5. RECIPROCITY
Give value before asking for anything.
- **Formula:** Free tip/guide $\rightarrow$ then CTA
- **Application:** Providing a free PT session or recovery guide before asking for a signup.

### 6. AUTHORITY
Establish premium positioning at an accessible price.
- **Formula:** "Perth's only gym with [X + Y + Z] under $12/week"
- **Application:** Highlighting the rare combo of Sauna, Ice Bath, Reformer Pilates, and Kids Hub at $11.95/week.

## Advanced Layers

### 7. SHAME $\rightarrow$ IDENTITY PROGRESSION
Strategically use mild shame to stop the scroll, then immediately pivot to an empowering identity.
- **Cold/Prospecting:** "Still putting everyone else first?" $\rightarrow$ "8,000 Perth mums chose themselves. You ready?"
- **Warm/Retargeting:** Zero shame. Pure community + identity. "You're already one of us. Just make it official."
- **Nurture/Email:**
  - Stage 1: Mild shame hook $\rightarrow$ identity pivot.
  - Stage 2: Pure community + social proof.
  - Stage 3: Urgency/Loss aversion only.

### 8. MICRO-COMMITMENT LADDER
Reduce friction by asking for small "yeses" before the big "join" ask.
- **Organic Stories:** Polls ("Would you try a free first visit? Yes/Maybe")
- **Organic Captions:** Engagement CTAs ("Comment BETTER if you want our free 7-day plan")
- **Link in Bio:** Low-pressure entry ("Book a free tour — no obligation, 5 minutes")
- **Ad Flow:** Cold Ad (Book Free Tour) $\rightarrow$ Retargeting Ad (Claim $0 Offer) $\rightarrow$ Final Call (Join Now).

### 9. GYMTIMIDATION REMOVAL
Proactively dismantle the fear of "not being fit enough" or "not fitting in."
- **Storytelling:** "Nervous about starting? So was [Member Name] from [Suburb]. She walked in not knowing anyone. Now she's here 4x a week."
- **Guarantee:** "First visit is always on us — no judgment, no pressure."
- **Ad Footer:** "No judgment. Every level welcome. First visit free."

## Objection Removal
Always address at least one of these common barriers:
- **"Too expensive"** $\rightarrow$ "$11.95/week = less than one coffee a day"
- **"Might not go"** $\rightarrow$ "No lock-in — only pay when you want"
- **"Feel judged"** $\rightarrow$ "8,000 members, every fitness level, zero judgment"
- **"FIFO schedule"** $\rightarrow$ "Freeze anytime. Your membership works around your roster."
- **"No time"** $\rightarrow$ "24/7 access — 3am or 3pm, we're open"
