# Brand Voice — ChasingBetter247

## Identity
- Name: ChasingBetter247
- Tagline: AlwaysBetter
- Sub-brands: Neon21 | ChasingRX | Reformer Pilates | Kids Hub
- Slogans: **You Vs You** · **Always Better** · **Chasing Better**

---

## What CB247 Sells
Not a gym membership. An experience.

- A different kind of gym
- Great value with premium facilities
- One-stop shop for wellness
- Elite fitness for affordable prices

> "Brand is not what you say it is. It's what THEY say it is." — Marty Neumeier

CB247's brand is built on the experience members have — not the list of features we publish. Every piece of content asks: **what does this feel like?**

---

## Voice

- Direct, warm, energetic, Perth-local
- Edgy, artsy, surprising — like a fringe festival with barbells
- Never corporate, never passive voice
- Write like a coach talking to a mate, not a brand talking to a customer
- Comfortable, not anxious. Intense, not intimidating.

---

## Language Rules

USE: "Train" not "exercise"  
USE: "Members" not "customers"  
USE: "Join" not "sign up" (unless CTA)  
NEVER: "leverage", "synergy", "utilize", "facilitate"  
NEVER: passive voice  
NEVER: list facilities in social feed posts — show the experience instead  
NEVER: reference competitors by name in any published content

---

## Tone by Content Type

| Channel | Direction |
|---------|-----------|
| **Instagram Feed** | Experience-first. Show the vibe, the people, the feeling. GymBox-style — high contrast, edgy, surprising. NO direct product pitches. |
| **Instagram Reels** | High energy, punchy, 3-second hook. Real members in real moments. Diverse. Artsy. Unexpected angles. |
| **TikTok** | Raw, authentic, surprising. Reaction-style, behind-the-scenes, reveal formats. Never over-produced. |
| **Instagram Stories** | OK for direct product/facility callouts ("we have saunas/ice baths"). Short-lived so product push is fine here. |
| **Facebook** | Community-first, warm, conversational |
| **EDM / SMS** | Product and facility announcements live here — "We have saunas/ice baths/lockers" is EDM content, NOT feed content |
| **Google Ads** | Benefit-led, price anchor, urgency without desperation |
| **Email** | Personal, direct, short — max 150 words |
| **GBP Posts** | Local SEO-focused — OK to mention facilities and price directly. Experience language preferred but product info is appropriate. |
| **Blogs / SEO** | Educational, data-backed, humanizer-processed. Facilities mentioned as context, not pitch. |

---

## The Social/EDM Rule (CRITICAL)

**Feed = sell experience. EDM/SMS = sell product.**

Facility features (sauna, ice bath, lockers, Kids Hub, Reformer Pilates beds) belong in:
- EDM and SMS to members
- Instagram/Facebook Stories
- GBP posts

They do NOT belong as direct pitches in the main Instagram or TikTok feed. Show those facilities through experience content instead — real people, real moments, high-contrast visuals.

---

## Aesthetic Direction

**Inspired by: GymBox (London), Gym80**

- High contrast, edgy colours, **unnatural** lighting — not natural/airy
- Neon, glow, filtered
- Diverse range of people — NOT just fitness models
- Fringe festival energy — artsy, unexpected, a bit surprising
- Emotions to evoke: fun, different/unique, inspired, comfortable (not anxious), intense (not intimidating), **surprise**

**Avoid:**
- Clean, natural, corporate aesthetics
- Sensual imagery
- Stock-photo fitness models
- Bland white-background product shots
