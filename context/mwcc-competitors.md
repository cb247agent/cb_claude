## Notes per competitor

### Midvale Hub
- Only true geo-competitor (Midvale suburb)
- Lower volume but high local SEO presence
- Watch for: Midvale-specific keyword rankings, Google Business Profile review momentum

### Goodstart
- Non-profit positioning (cheaper fees, trust signals)
- Massive content footprint
- Watch for: long-tail informational queries (e.g., "how to choose childcare")

### Nido Early School
- Premium positioning (modern centres, educator-led pedagogy)
- Higher-end pricing
- Watch for: positioning around "quality educators" + "play-based learning"

### Aggregator threats (Care for Kids + KindiCare)
- These dominate SERPs for "childcare near me" + "childcare Perth" searches
- MWCC needs to rank for centre-specific long-tail (e.g., "childcare Midvale", "OSHC Rockingham") to bypass aggregator dominance
- Worth listing MWCC on these directories for backlinks (not for direct lead acquisition — their conversion rate is poor)
