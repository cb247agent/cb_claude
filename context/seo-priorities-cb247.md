# SEO Priorities – ChasingBetter247 (Pilot Phase)

## Timeframe
Phase: Pilot AI SEO Agent  
Period: Q2 2026 (April–June), focusing on Malaga & Ellenbrook, plus Cockburn pre-launch.

## Business Objectives
1. Increase new memberships for Malaga & Ellenbrook from organic search.
2. Position CB247 as the premium recovery + performance gym in Perth (sauna, ice bath, massage, bath house).
3. Build early awareness and waitlist demand for Cockburn before opening.

## SEO Objectives (Phase 1)
1. MALAGA
   - Win on “gym malaga” and “24/7 gym malaga” search terms.
   - Capture recovery intent: “sauna malaga”, “ice bath malaga”, “bath house malaga”.
   - Promote Kids Hub: “kids gym malaga”, “childminding malaga gym”.

2. ELLENBROOK
   - Solidify presence for “gym ellenbrook” and “24/7 gym ellenbrook”.
   - Focus on family + community angles (“family gym”, “kids friendly gym”).

3. COCKBURN (PRE-LAUNCH)
   - Create a pre-launch landing page for “gym cockburn” and “bath house cockburn”.
   - Drive awareness and email list / waitlist sign-ups.

## KPI Priorities (High-Level)
- Ranking movement for key local terms (top 3 / top 5 visibility).
- Organic traffic to:
  - Join/membership pages per location.
  - Kids Hub pages.
  - Recovery pages (sauna, ice bath, massage, bath house).
- Leads / form fills coming from organic (where trackable later).

For now (pilot), focus on:
- Quality and completeness of SEO recommendations.
- Quality of draft content produced by the AI agent.
- Clarity of weekly SEO report for stakeholders.

## Constraints & Rules
- NO auto-publishing: all changes must be reviewed and implemented by humans.
- Prioritise fixes and content with:
  - High business impact (membership, recovery, kids).
  - Reasonable effort for the team to implement.
- Use existing tooling first (web data, public pages). GA4 / GSC / APIs can be added later.
