# Marketing Strategy — ChasingBetter247

## Goals
Primary: New membership sign-ups (target: 10+/week per location)
Secondary: Brand awareness Malaga/Ellenbrook + NOR Perth

## Channels (priority order)
1. Meta Ads — primary paid channel
2. Google Search Ads — intent capture
3. Instagram Organic @chasingbetter247 — community
4. Google Business Profile — local SEO
5. Email/SMS — lead nurture + retention

## 5 ICPs
1. FIFO Worker — WA mining, 28-50, irregular schedule, Malaga/Ellenbrook area
2. Young Local Family — 28-42, kids, needs Kids Hub, Ellenbrook focus
3. Fitness Newcomer — 20-45, intimidated, wants welcoming gym
4. Serious Athlete — 25-40, premium equipment + Neon21/ChasingRX
5. Recovery-Focused — 35-55, sauna/ice bath primary driver

Google: CTR>4% | CPC<$3 | Lead CVR>5%
