Don't Quit Winter — Training Brief (Brand Manager + Reception)

What this campaign is — in 30 seconds

We have 279 unique members in the future-cancel pipeline (they've submitted cancellation but haven't left yet). That's 4× this week's new-join rate. If we save 30% with one phone call each, that's 84 members preserved = ~$4,800/mo recurring revenue at zero acquisition cost. The offer is simple: freeze instead of cancel, and we cover free Recovery (Sauna + Ice Bath) for a month when you come back.

This is the single highest-ROI activity on the calendar this winter. It outperforms paid acquisition because:

  • CAC for a new member ≈ $80–120 paid · $0 here
  • Saved members have ~30% higher LTV than new joins (already onboarded)
  • Recovery upsell after the free month adds ~$20/wk recurring on top

Roles

Brand Manager

  • Owns the campaign + weekly save-rate KPI
  • Reviews 5 random calls per week for branch-fit (see playbook §7)
  • Handles escalations (hardship cases)
  • Posts weekly headline in team channel
  • Verdicts the auto-promoted action card in Performance Review at +14 days

Reception (Malaga + Ellenbrook)

  • Runs the calls 11:00–14:00 Mon–Fri during quiet windows
  • 2 calls every 15 min target = ~8 calls/shift each
  • Logs outcome + reason tags in PGM after every call
  • Flags genuine hardship to Brand Manager same day

Asset Creator (background)

  • 3× reels, 4× stories, 1× EDM tied to this campaign (see Asset Library)
  • Captured at next monthly shoot (June 18 Ellenbrook)
  • Posters for reception desk (quick-reference card from §9 of full playbook)

What we WILL offer

  • ✅ Freeze (4-week minimum, no charges)
  • ✅ Free Recovery (Sauna + Ice Bath) for 4 weeks on return
  • ✅ Same locked-in rate when they reactivate
  • ✅ 28-day check-in calendar invite

What we WILL NOT offer

  • ❌ Permanent rate discount
  • ❌ Free months of base membership (that's acquisition)
  • ❌ Personal training sessions (separate add-on)
  • ❌ Waiver of future fees
  • ❌ Anything that's not in the playbook

If a member negotiates hard for something not on the list → "I genuinely can't authorise that. Let me put you on freeze while I see if my manager can do anything." Escalate to Brand Manager same day. Do not promise.

Weekly Friday 16:00 — Brand Manager review

15-minute meeting. Open the Membership page on the dashboard. Pull these numbers:

  1. Save rate this week (Save Rate KPI card · target ≥ 30%)
  2. Pool remaining (future-cancel pipeline)
  3. Top branch used (which reason came up most)
  4. Any calls flagged for review (5 random + any escalations)
  5. Recovery upsell post-freeze (track conversion from SAVED-FREEZE → paid Recovery)

Post the one-line headline to the team channel:

"Week N: 87 reached, 28 saved, 32% rate. Top reason: Not Using. Pool down to 192. Strong week."

What "good" looks like by end of Week 4

  • Pool reduced from 279 → ~155 (30% saved + natural attrition)
  • Save Rate KPI on Membership page reads 30%+
  • Recovery add-on subscribers up from 19 → ~50 (post-freeze upsell)
  • Performance Review auto-promoted card verdict-ed as "winner" or "partial_win"
  • Next month's emitter repeats the archetype (Wave 6 feedback loop) with a fresh variation

If we hit "winner" verdict, the Promo Concept Generator will keep this archetype running in July + August. If we hit "underperforming", it gets retired and we try a different lever. The system learns either way.

Escalation rules

Same-day escalation to Brand Manager:

  • Member visibly distressed during call (health, job loss, relationship)
  • Member demands rate discount
  • Member threatens to leave a negative review
  • Member is on a corporate / partner account (different cancellation pathway)
  • Call recording flagged by member as a complaint

End-of-week escalation:

  • Branch-fit accuracy < 60% across reception staff (need re-training)
  • Save rate < 15% in week 1 (rethink offer or pool quality)
  • Recovery upsell rate < 25% post-freeze (Recovery experience may need work)

Quick-reference checklist (laminate for desk)

  • [ ] Opened warmly, used first name twice
  • [ ] Asked "What changed since you joined?" — didn't pitch first
  • [ ] Listened ≥ 30 sec before offering
  • [ ] Matched branch to actual reason (not the survey answer)
  • [ ] Offered freeze + Recovery in one sentence
  • [ ] Closed with admin framing, not emotional framing
  • [ ] Logged outcome + reason tag in PGM same day
  • [ ] Calendar invite 28-day check-in if SAVED-FREEZE
