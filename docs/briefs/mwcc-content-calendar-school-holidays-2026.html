Why this 8-week plan exists

Three audiences search at the same time around school holidays: Vacation Care families (need OSHC holiday cover), Term 3 OSHC families (need before/after care starting 20 Jul), and LDC researching parents (pause work, research childcare for term 3 + 2027 kindy). One coordinated calendar serves all three.

Network goal: lift occupancy ~51% → 56% by 31 Jul (5-point lift ≈ 20–25 enrolments).
