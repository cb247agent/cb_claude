Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a gym membership to use the Recovery Hub?

Yes — the Recovery Hub (sauna, ice bath, massage) at CB247 Malaga is an included benefit of your gym membership. You don't need a separate recovery membership or pay-per-session for the sauna and ice bath. Just show up and use it.

How long can I stay in the sauna or ice bath?

For the sauna, we recommend 10–20 minutes per session — listen to your body. For the ice bath, start with 3–5 minutes and work your way up as your cold tolerance improves. There's no hard limit; just train smart and step out if something doesn't feel right.

Can I book a massage in advance at CB247 Malaga?

Yes — massage sessions can be booked in advance via our Timetable page or by asking our team at reception. We offer sports massage, remedial massage, and recovery massage. Sessions fill up, especially on weekends, so booking ahead is a good idea.

What should I bring to a recovery session?

Just bring yourself and a towel — that's it. CB247 provides everything else you need for the sauna and ice bath. For massage sessions, wear comfortable clothing and let your therapist know if you have any problem areas they should focus on.

Is the Recovery Hub suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. The Recovery Hub isn't just for elite athletes. If you're new to training, the sauna is a great way to ease muscle soreness as your body adapts. Start with shorter sessions and lower temperatures — there's no rush. Recovery is for everyone.
