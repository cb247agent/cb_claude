What ages are accepted in the Kids Hub at CB247 Malaga?

The Kids Hub at CB247 Malaga welcomes children from toddlers through to around 12 years old. Age-appropriate activities and supervision ratios are maintained to keep every child safe and engaged while parents train.

Do I need to book in advance for the Kids Hub?

Yes — we recommend booking your Kids Hub session in advance through our Timetable page. Spots can fill up quickly, especially during school holidays and peak morning periods. Booking ahead means you won't miss out.

How are children supervised in the Kids Hub?

Every Kids Hub session is supervised by trained staff who genuinely enjoy working with kids. We maintain strict child-to-staff ratios, and all team members are checked and trained in child safety. Your kids are in good hands.

What happens if my child is unwell?

If your child is showing signs of illness — fever, runny nose, tummy issues — please keep them home until they're feeling better. This protects all the kids in our care. When in doubt, give us a call before heading in.
