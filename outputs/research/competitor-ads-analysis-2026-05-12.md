# Competitor Ad Intelligence: Winter Attack 2026

## Competitor Landscape Analysis
*Date: 2026-05-12 | Campaign: Winter Attack 2026 | Focus: Competitor Attack*

### 1. Revo Fitness (Primary Threat)
- **Current Offers:** Likely $9.69/week (Simple) or $12.69/week (Level Two).
- **Creative Angles:** Minimalism, "No Lock-in", "Open 24/7", "Reformer Pilates" (Level Two focus).
- **CB247 Advantage:** CB247 matches the $11.95/week price point but includes **Sauna + Ice Bath + Kids Hub** which Revo lacks entirely. CB247's Reformer is included in the base membership ($11.95) vs Revo's "Level Two" tier.

### 2. Ryderwear Gym Malaga (Location Threat)
- **Current Offers:** Premium pricing, often focuses on apparel/influencer aesthetic.
- **Creative Angles:** High-end equipment, "Best gym in Malaga", lifting culture.
- **CB247 Advantage:** **Price** (CB247 is significantly cheaper) and **Facilities** (Reformer, Sauna, Ice Bath, Kids Hub). Ryderwear is "lifter-only" focused; CB247 is "lifestyle + family + recovery".

### 3. Anytime Fitness / Snap Fitness (Awareness Threat)
- **Current Offers:** Usually ~$15-18/week + joining fees + key fob fees.
- **Creative Angles:** Global network, convenience, "join one join all".
- **CB247 Advantage:** **Price anchor ($11.95)** vs $15+. **Facilities** — Anytime/Snap are basic cardio/weights gyms. CB247 is a "Wellness Hub". **FIFO-specific freeze** — AF/Snap offer standard freezes but CB247 owns the local FIFO market.

### 4. Competitive Differentiation Strategy
- **The Hook:** "Why pay $15/week for half the facilities?"
- **The Anchor:** $11.95/week. No lock-in.
- **The Attack:** Targeted ads at people visiting "Revo Malaga" or searching for "Gym Malaga".
- **FIFO Power Move:** Highlight the FIFO-friendly freeze which competitors don't promote or handle as well locally.
