# Executive Report: CB_Marketing Tool Stack Investment Proposal

## Executive Summary
This proposal delineates the annual investment requirement for the CB_Marketing system, an AI-driven automation platform designed to support ChasingBetter Group across 5 businesses. Implementation of this infrastructure will shift manual, site-specific operations to a unified, scalable automated ecosystem.

## Financial Overview
The proposed annual investment is **$4,260**, distributed across three tiers of functionality:
*   **Essential Infrastructure:** $2,724/annum (Core automation)
*   **Team Collaboration:** $960/annum (System access & reporting)
*   **Operational Optimization:** $576/annum (Documentation/Integration)

## Strategic Impact
Transitioning to this tech stack provides significant operational elasticity:
*   **Operational Scalability:** Multi-site support (from 1 to 5 locations) without incremental staffing.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Automation of SEO content generation (est. 4-6 hours/week savings).
*   **Competitive Intelligence:** Implementation of systematic market monitoring.
*   **Decision Support:** Data-driven KPI tracking and management insights.

## Tactical Roadmap
The rollout is structured for risk management and incremental validation:
1.  **Phase I:** Core automation (OpenRouter/Apify) to validate operational throughput.
2.  **Phase II:** SEO visibility expansion via Ahrefs integration.
3.  **Phase III:** Content production capacity increase with Higgsfield.
4.  **Phase IV:** Full team enablement via seat expansion.

## Recommendation
Immediate authorization of Phase I is recommended to baseline current operational metrics and initiate the transition process.
