Strategic Insights

What the Data Tells Us

Prioritised observations and recommended actions based on this week's data across all channels.

High Priority

Google Ads — Malaga CPA Crisis

Malaga CPA jumped from $15.12 to $78.65 (+420% WoW) while Ellenbrook CPA improved to $19.69. With only 4 conversions from 166 clicks, Malaga campaigns need an urgent audit — likely ad creative fatigue or audience mismatch.

Monitor

Total Sessions Down 20.5%

Overall sessions dropped significantly vs prior week. Paid social and cross-network channels account for most of the decline. Cross-network has driven 398 sessions with zero conversions — investigate attribution routing.

Opportunity

Direct Traffic Converts 47%

Direct traffic (509 sessions → 240 conversions) has a 47% conversion rate — 3× the site average. This reflects strong brand recall. Amplify brand awareness campaigns to grow this high-intent segment.

Opportunity

#1 Position in Organic Search

CB247 holds position 1.1–1.2 for all primary branded queries with 36.6% CTR. This is exceptional brand dominance in local search. Protect it — ensure Google Business Profile is fully optimised.

Monitor

Mobile UX is Everything

82.5% of sessions are mobile. The Reformer Pilates page (top non-homepage page) and Contact page (3rd most visited) should be the priority mobile conversion optimisations this week.

High Priority

Sauna + Ice Bath Invisible

CB247's unique differentiator (sauna + ice bath combo) doesn't appear in any top page data. This is a major gap — prospects searching for these features likely won't find CB247. Add a dedicated landing page and include in SEO content plan.
