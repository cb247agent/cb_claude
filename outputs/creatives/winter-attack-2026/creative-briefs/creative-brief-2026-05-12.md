# Creative Brief: Winter Attack 2026

## Overview
- **Campaign:** Winter Attack 2026
- **Objective:** Acquisition / Competitor Attack
- **Brand Essence:** AlwaysBetter. Local, energetic, premium-for-less.

---

## 1. Visual Style Guide
- **Colors:** Deep Black (BG), High-Vis Neon/White (Text), Cold Blue (Recovery highlights).
- **Typography:** Bold, heavy san-serif. Headlines should hit hard.
- **Vibe:** "Raw but clean". High-quality gym footage, not stock photos.

---

## 2. Image Asset Concepts

### Concept A: The "Stack" (Price vs Facilities)
- **Visual:** Split screen. Left side: Boring basic gym photo. Right side: High-energy CB247 shot (Sauna/Reformer).
- **Text Overlay:** "Them: $15+ / Us: $11.95"
- **AI Prompt (Ideogram):** High-contrast split screen photo. Left side is a dimly lit dated gym. Right side is a modern luxury fitness club with a sauna and reformer pilates machines. Text overlay in bold neon letters: "STOP OVERPAYING".
- **Format:** 1080x1080 (Feed).

### Concept B: FIFO Life
- **Visual:** A worker in high-vis gear dropping their bag at the Malaga gym entrance.
- **Text Overlay:** "WFH? Nah, WFS (Work From Site)."
- **AI Prompt (Ideogram):** A muscular Australian man in high-visibility work gear walking into a modern 24/7 gym at night. Neon signs, high contrast, cinematic lighting.
- **Format:** 1080x1350 (Feed/Portait).

### Concept C: Recovery Focus
- **Visual:** Close up of someone breathing in the Ice Bath or laughing in the Sauna.
- **Text Overlay:** "Sauna Included - $11.95/wk"
- **AI Prompt (Ideogram):** Atmospheric photo of a person in a modern wooden sauna room, steam rising, backlit with warm orange light. Text in clean white font: "SAUNA INCLUDED".
- **Format:** 1080x1920 (Story/Reels).

---

## 3. Video Asset Concept: "The Switch" (Reels/TikTok)

### Storyboard
1. **0-3s (The Hook):** POV of someone holding a gym key tag for "Anytime" or "Revo" looking frustrated. Text: "Paying for a basic gym?"
2. **3-7s (The Transition):** Fast cut (match cut) to them scanning into CB247 Malaga. Energetic music drop.
3. **7-12s (The Montage):** 0.5s clips: Heavy lifting → Reformer stretch → Cold plunge → Sauna steam → Kids Hub laugh.
4. **12-15s (The Close):** Static card: $11.95/week. No Lock-in. JOIN NOW.

### Technical Specs
- **Music:** Phonk or High-energy local hip-hop.
- **Captions:** Native Reels/TikTok font, yellow/white.
- **Ratio:** 9:16.
