# Creative Brief: "The Better Choice" Competitor-Attack Campaign
Date: 2026-04-29
Campaign Objective: High-impact visual contrast between "Basic Budget Gyms" and the "Premium CB247 Experience."

---

## General Visual Guidelines
- **Colors:** Primary #3FA69A (Teal) and #0a0a0a (Deep Black).
- **Vibe:** Raw, energetic, authentic. Avoid stock photos; use real member footage or high-quality a-roll of the Malaga facility.
- **Text Overlays:** Bold, sans-serif, high contrast. Key phrases should pop in #3FA69A.

---

## Angle 1: The "Parent's Paradise"
**Goal:** Visual relief. Transition from "Gym Stress" to "Gym Peace."

### 🎥 Reel/Video Concept
- **Scene 1 (0-3s):** [Chaos] Fast cuts of a toddler screaming in a car seat or a parent trying to do a bicep curl with a kid hanging off their leg. B&W filter.
- **Scene 2 (3-7s):** [The Pivot] Sudden shift to color. Slow-mo shot of the Kids Hub door opening, child happily entering with a staff member.
- **Scene 3 (7-12s):** [The Win] Close-up of the parent hitting a PR or breathing deeply in a yoga pose, looking focused and calm.
- **Music:** Fast/chaotic audio $\rightarrow$ Smooth, upbeat lo-fi beat.

### 🖼️ Static Image Concept
- **Split Screen:** Left side (B&W) "The Struggle" (Blurred image of a chaotic living room). Right side (Full Color) "The Solution" (Bright, clean shot of the Kids Hub interior).
- **Overlay:** "STOP CHOOSING BETWEEN PARENTING & FITNESS."

---

## Angle 2: The "Premium Recovery"
**Goal:** Aspirational and visceral. Make the viewer "feel" the cold and heat.

### 🎥 Reel/Video Concept
- **Scene 1 (0-3s):** [The Hook] First-person POV diving into the Ice Bath. Sharp "gasp" audio.
- **Scene 2 (3-7s):** [The Contrast] Cut to the steam and warmth of the Sauna. Slow, cinematic pan of the facility.
- **Scene 3 (7-12s):** [The Value] Text overlay pops: "S$50/week facilities. $11.95/week price."
- **Music:** Deep, atmospheric bass pulsing.

### 🖼️ Static Image Concept
- **Hero Shot:** A high-contrast shot of someone mid-plunge in the Ice Bath, water droplets frozen in air. 
- **Overlay:** "ELITE RECOVERY. BUDGET PRICE." (Teal text on black background).

---

## Angle 3: The "FIFO Freedom"
**Goal:** Relatability. Respect the grind, remove the stress.

### 🎥 Reel/Video Concept
- **Scene 1 (0-3s):** [The Pain] Shot of a phone screen showing a gym membership payment notification while the person is in hi-vis gear on a mine site.
- **Scene 2 (3-7s):** [The Solution] Cut to a finger tapping the "Freeze Account" button on a phone.
- **Scene 3 (7-12s):** [The Return] Shot of the member walking back into the gym in Malaga, greeting a mate.
- **Music:** Rhythmic, steady "work" beat.

### 🖼️ Static Image Concept
- **Image:** A pair of work boots and a gym bag side-by-side. 
- **Overlay:** "Your gym should work around your roster. Not the other way around."
- **Sub-text:** "FIFO Friendly Freeze. No Lock-ins."

---

## Angle 4: The "Value Smash"
**Goal:** Logical superiority. The "No-Brainer" offer.

### 🎥 Reel/Video Concept
- **Scene 1 (0-3s):** [The Comparison] Rapid-fire clips of "Basic" gym equipment (a single rusty dumbbell, a crowded line for a machine).
- **Scene 2 (3-8s):** [The Upgrade] Cinematic montage of CB247: Reformer Pilates $\rightarrow$ Sauna $\rightarrow$ Ice Bath $\rightarrow$ Kids Hub.
- **Scene 3 (8-12s):** [The Price] Big bold text: "$11.95/week."
- **Music:** Fast, high-energy "Comparison" style audio (Tik Tok trend style).

### 🖼️ Static Image Concept
- **Checklist Graphic:** 
  - Basic Gym: [X] Weights [ ] Sauna [ ] Ice Bath [ ] Kids Hub
  - CB247: [✓] Weights [✓] Sauna [✓] Ice Bath [✓] Kids Hub
- **Bottom Text:** "Same Price. Way More Stuff."
