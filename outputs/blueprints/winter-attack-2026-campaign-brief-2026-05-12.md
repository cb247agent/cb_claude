# Campaign Brief: Winter Attack 2026

## 1. Overview
- **Campaign Name:** Winter Attack 2026
- **Objective:** Competitor Attack / Acquisition (Switch from Revo/Anytime/Boutique Pilates)
- **Start Date:** 2026-05-12
- **Duration:** 30 Days (Full Month Attack)
- **Locations:** Malaga + Ellenbrook
- **Primary Offer:** $11.95/week, NO Lock-in, 1st Week FREE + $0 Joining Fee.
- **Inclusions:** 24/7 Access, Unlimited Reformer Pilates, Sauna, Ice Bath, Kids Hub, Neon21 HIIT.
- **Budget:** ~$1,100/week ($4,400 total)
- **KPIs:** 300+ New Leads, 150+ Sign-ups (target $30 CPA).

## 2. Target Audience (ICPs)
- **Primary ICP: The FIFO Worker**
    - **Pain Points:** Paying for gym while on-site, limited recovery options.
    - **Hook:** $0 membership freezes while you're away + premium recovery when you're back.
- **Secondary ICP: The Cost-Conscious Fitness Switcher**
    - **Pain Points:** Overpaying for boutique Pilates or bare-bones "big box" gyms.
    - **Hook:** Everything included for $11.95/wk. Cheaper than Revo/Anytime with 5x more facilities.

## 3. Brand Voice & Psychology
- **Voice:** Direct, coach-to-mate, Perth-local.
- **Triggers:**
    - **Loss Aversion:** Stop overpaying for boutique studios ($60/wk vs $11.95/wk).
    - **Authority:** Only gym with the "Big 5" (Pilates + Sauna + Ice Bath + Kids Hub + 24/7).
    - **Safety:** No Lock-in — you're in control.

## 4. Channel Strategy
- **Meta Ads (60%):** High-energy Reels and price-anchor image ads.
- **Google Search (20%):** High intent (e.g., "gym near malaga", "reformer pilates ellenbrook").
- **Email/SMS (10%):** Nurture sequence for database and abandoned leads.
- **Organic Social (10%):** Community proof and facility highlights.
