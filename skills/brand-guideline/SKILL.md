## Color Palette
Primary BG:   #0a0a0a   → Near black (main background)
Accent:       #3FA69A   → Teal green (primary brand color, CTAs, highlights)
Accent Dark:  #2d7a70   → Darker teal (hover states, borders)
Text Light:   #ffffff   → White (headings, primary text)
Text Muted:   #aaaaaa   → Grey (secondary text, captions)
Overlay:      rgba(63,166,154,0.15) → Teal glass effect for cards

## Typography
Heading:  Bold, ALL CAPS, modern sans-serif (Bebas Neue / Impact style)
Body:     Inter / DM Sans — clean, readable
Size H1:  48–64px for social graphics
Size H2:  32–40px
Body:     16–18px

## Logo Rules
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor logo
- Minimum clear space: 20px all sides
- Dark background preferred
- White version for any light background
- Sub-brands (Neon21, ChasingRX) use their own treatment

## Visual Style
- Dark backgrounds only for gym content
- Real members, real sweat — no stock photography
- Teal (#3FA69A) highlights for recovery/pilates content
- High contrast black + teal for Neon21 content
- Clean minimal teal + white for Reformer Pilates content
- Family-warm tone for Kids Hub content
