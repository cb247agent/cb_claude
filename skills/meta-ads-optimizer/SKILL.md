## 4-Ad-Set Structure

### Ad Set 1 — Cold Local (Radius Targeting)
**Audience:** 25–45, Malaga + Ellenbrook 10km radius, interests: fitness, gym, health
**Budget allocation:** 25% of cold spend
**Goal:** Awareness → Lead

**Variant A — Price Anchor:**
Primary: "Most gyms charge $15+/week and give you a room. We charge $11.95 and give you Sauna, Ice Bath, Reformer Pilates, Kids Hub. No lock-in. Malaga + Ellenbrook."
Headline: "$11.95/week. More Gym."
Variant B — Emotion (Family):
Primary: "You already show up for everyone else. Now show up for yourself. Sauna. Ice Bath. Reformer Pilates. Kids Hub on-site. CB247 — where Perth families train together."
Headline: "Train While the Kids Play."
Variant C — Social Proof:
Primary: "8,000+ Perth members chose CB247. Not because we're the cheapest — because we're the most complete. $11.95/week. No lock-in. Free first visit."
Headline: "8,000+ Perth Members."

### Ad Set 2 — Cold FIFO (Mining / Shift Workers)
**Audience:** 22–45, Mining towns WA (Pilbara, Goldfields), FIFO lifestyle interests
**Budget allocation:** 25% of cold spend
**Goal:** Conversion via FIFO freeze offer

**Variant A — Pain:**
Primary: "Paying for a gym you can't use while you're on site? Stop donating to corporate gyms. CB247's FIFO-friendly freeze means pay only when you're home. $11.95/week. No lock-in."
Headline: "Pay Only When You're Home."
Variant B — Identity:
Primary: "The only gym in Perth that understands the FIFO life. Train hard when you're home. Freeze when you're not. Simple. Fair. Local."
Headline: "The Only Gym That Gets FIFO."
Variant C — Price + Identity:
Primary: "CB247: $11.95/week, no lock-in. Sauna, Ice Bath, Reformer, 24/7 access. The most complete gym in Malaga — for less than a movie ticket."
Headline: "More Gym. Less Money."

### Ad Set 3 — Warm 90-Day Engagers
**Audience:** Instagram + Facebook engagers (last 90 days), video views >3s
**Budget allocation:** 30% of warm spend
**Goal:** Convert engaged non-members

**Variant A — Soft Offer:**
Primary: "You've been watching for a while. CB247 is a 24/7 gym with Sauna, Ice Bath, Reformer Pilates, and a Kids Hub. $11.95/week. No lock-in. Your first visit is on us."
Headline: "Your First Visit Is Free."
Variant B — Social Proof + Urgency:
Primary: "8,000+ Perth members chose CB247. Most of them were 'just looking' too. The difference? They actually showed up. Free visit. Offer ends [date]."
Headline: "8,000+ Members Can't Be Wrong."
Variant C — Identity + Facility:
Primary: "You're the type who trains properly. You need proper recovery. Sauna + Ice Bath + Reformer Pilates. Not a globo gym. $11.95/week. No lock-in. Join the ones who know."
Headline: "Train Properly. Recover Better."

### Ad Set 4 — Retargeting 30-Day (Website Visitors)
**Audience:** Website visitors (last 30 days), did not convert
**Budget allocation:** 20% of retargeting spend
**Goal:** Close the loop — free PT session offer

**Variant A — Free PT:**
Primary: "You visited CB247 — and didn't book. Here's what most visitors miss: a free PT session with one of our trainers. They'll show you around, build you a program, and answer every question. 45 minutes. On the house."
Headline: "Free PT Session. No Obligation."
Variant B — Last Chance:**
Primary: "You looked. You saved. You hesitated. The $0 joining fee offer for May ends [date]. After that, standard fees apply. Don't let another week pass."
Headline: "[Date] — Your Last Chance."
Variant C — No-Risk:**
Primary: "No lock-in. Cancel anytime. Free first visit. If CB247 isn't the right fit after your tour, you lose nothing. But if it is — you just made the best decision of your year."
Headline: "Zero Risk. All Reward."

## UTM Construction (Meta Ads)

```
utm_source=meta
utm_medium=paid_social
utm_campaign=[objective]-[location]-[month]-[year]
utm_content=[format]-[variant]-[ad-set]
```

**Example Cold FIFO:**
```
https://chasingbetter247.com.au/join?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=membership-perth-may-2026&utm_content=static-variant-a-fifo
```

**Example Retargeting:**
```
https://chasingbetter247.com.au/join?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=membership-perth-may-2026&utm_content=static-variant-a-retargeting
```

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Primary text under 125 characters (count)
- [ ] Headline under 27 characters (count)
- [ ] All UTMs lowercase, no spaces
- [ ] Landing page URL correct (not homepage — specific page)
- [ ] All 4 UTM parameters present (source, medium, campaign, content)
- [ ] Price anchor ($11.95/week) in every ad
- [ ] "No lock-in" in every ad
- [ ] No corporate language (no "leverage", "utilize", "synergy")
- [ ] "Train" not "exercise" — brand voice check
- [ ] No stock photo descriptions — specify real member or facility shot
- [ ] FIFO freeze mentioned when targeting FIFO audience
- [ ] Kids Hub mentioned when targeting families
- [ ] Each variant has distinct hook — not same angle copy-pasted
