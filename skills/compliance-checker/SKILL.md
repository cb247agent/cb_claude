## Australian Compliance Framework

### Key Regulatory Bodies
| Body | Jurisdiction | What They Regulate |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| **AANA** | Industry self-regulation | All advertising (code of ethics) |
| **ACCC** | Consumer law | Misleading conduct, testimonials |
| **TGA** | Therapeutic Goods | Health/medical claims |
| **ASA** | Alcohol | Spirits advertising |
| **oAAC** | Outdoor | Outdoor advertising sites |

---

## AANA Code of Ethics — Core Provisions

### Section 2: Truth and Accuracy
- **Must NOT** misrepresent: price, value, quality, availability
- **Must NOT** make false testimonials
- **Must NOT** mislead about "result" claims

### Section 3: Clear and Honest Representation
- No concealment of material facts
- No bait-and-switch advertising
- All offers must be genuine

### Section 4: Use of Testimonials
- Must be genuine, current, representative
- Must reflect genuine experience
- Cannot make therapeutic claims unless pre-approved
- Must have consent from testimonial provider

### Section 6: Safety
- Must not depict dangerous activities without adequate disclaimers
- Fitness content: "consult your doctor before starting"

### Section 7: Particular Categories
- **Discrimination:** No ads depicting discriminatory behavior
- **Gender:** Cannot demean based on gender (important for fitness marketing)

## ACL (Australian Consumer Law) — Key Rules

### False or Misleading Representations (s29)
Cannot claim:
- "Only gym with..." unless verified
- "% discount" unless from genuine original price
- "Award winning" unless documentation exists

### Unsolicited Consumer Agreements
- No pressure tactics in lead nurturing
- Easy unsubscribe required in all email/SMS

---

## Fitness Industry Specific Rules

### No Therapeutic Claims Without TGA Approval
| CANNOT Say | Alternative |
|-----------|-------------|
| "Heals" / "cures" | "Supports recovery" |
| "Treats" / "manages" | "May help with" |
| "Burns fat" | "Supports fitness goals" |
| "Lose Xkg in Y weeks" | "Supports your fitness journey" |
| "Proven to reduce..." | "Many members report feeling..." |

### Before/After Claims
- Must be typical results, not exceptional
- Must include disclaimer: "Results vary. Individual circumstances affect outcomes."
- Must not imply guaranteed results

### Membership Claims
- "No lock-in" — ONLY if genuinely cancel-anytime
- "$X/week" — Must be the total ongoing cost
- "Free" — Must have no hidden costs

## Compliance Checker Questions

For every piece of content, ask:

### Truth & Accuracy
- [ ] Can we substantiate every claim made?
- [ ] Are all prices, discounts, and offers accurate?
- [ ] Is "only" or "first" language verified?
- [ ] Are testimonials genuine and current?

### Safety & Responsibility
- [ ] Does content show dangerous stunts without warnings?
- [ ] Is professional advice disclaimer needed?
- [ ] Could content encourage unsafe fitness practices?

### Legal Claims
- [ ] Any therapeutic or health claims that need TGA approval?
- [ ] Are "typical results" disclaimers included?
- [ ] Is membership pricing fully transparent?

### Privacy & Spam
- [ ] Is consent captured before adding to email lists?
- [ ] Is unsubscribe easy in all communications?
- [ ] Are we complying with Australian Privacy Principles?

## CB247 Membership Structure — Add-Ons (CRITICAL)

The following services are NOT included in the base CB247 membership. They require an extra fee on top of the base membership. **Flag any content that calls these "included", "standard", "part of membership", or implies no extra cost:**

| Service | Correct Description |
|---------|-------------------|
| Sauna + ice bath | Add-on, extra fee. Available at both Malaga and Ellenbrook. |
| Kids Hub | Add-on, extra fee. Supervised childminding during staffed hours. |
| Reformer Pilates | Add-on, extra fee. |
| ChasingRX | Add-on, extra fee. |
| Yoga | Add-on, extra fee. |
| Spin | Add-on, extra fee. |

**What IS included in base membership:** 24/7 gym access, Neon21, general floor equipment, functional training area.

**Competitor comparison rule:** Never criticise competitors for "hidden costs" or "add-on fees" in any published content — CB247 also charges extra for add-ons. Only compare on verifiable differentiators (e.g. Kids Hub, sauna, ice bath — which competitors don't have).

## Common Fixes for CB247 Content

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Burns fat" | → "Supports your fitness goals" |
| "Lose weight fast" | → "Work towards your fitness goals" |
| "Heals sore muscles" | → "Supports muscle recovery" |
| "Only gym with..." | → Verify first, or reword to "One of Perth's few gyms with..." |
| No pricing disclaimer | → Add "$11.95/week. No lock-in. No signup fee." |
| Unsubstantiated testimonial | → Add "Results vary" disclaimer |
| "Sauna/ice bath/Kids Hub/Pilates/ChasingRX/yoga/spin included" | → "Available as an add-on — extra fee applies" |
| "All included in one membership" | → Remove. List only what base membership actually covers. |
