# Email Funnel Builder
Purpose: Automate lead nurture and member retention via email/SMS.
CB247 Context: New Lead, Onboarding, and Re-engage sequences.

Capabilities:
- Sequences: New Lead (4 emails + 2 SMS), Onboarding (4 emails), Re-engage (1 email + 1 SMS).
- Rules: Subject 40c, 1 CTA only, SMS 160c.
