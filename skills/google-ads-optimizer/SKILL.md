## Ad Copy Formulas (Search — Immediate Conversion Focus)

### Formula A — Price Anchor + Offer
```
Headline: [Facility Keyword] — $11.95/wk
Description: [Offer] + [differentiator] + [CTA]
Example: "Reformer Pilates Studio — $11.95/wk. Free first visit. No lock-in. Join CB247 Malaga."
```

```
Headline: FIFO? Freeze Your Gym.
```

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1 — Brand Search
**Objective:** Protect branded search traffic from competitors
**Daily budget:** $10–15
**Bid strategy:** Target CPA (if >20 conversions/week) or Max clicks
**Keywords:** Brand terms only (CB247, chasing better perth, etc.)
**Ad copy:** Price anchor unnecessary — focus on facilities + CTA

### Campaign 2 — Non-Brand Local (Priority)
**Objective:** Intercept gym searchers in Malaga + Ellenbrook area
**Daily budget:** $25–40
**Bid strategy:** Target CPA (~$15-20 AUD)
**Keywords:** Gym comparison, facility, price searches
**Ad copy:** Lead with price anchor + location + primary facility
**Location targeting:** Malaga 15km + Ellenbrook 15km (combined)
**Ad schedule:** Run 24/7 initially, optimize after 2 weeks

### Campaign 3 — Performance Max (Broad + Retargeting)
**Objective:** Awareness + mid-funnel across YouTube, Gmail, Display
**Daily budget:** $15–20
**Asset groups:** Need 3 image assets + logo + headline + description per asset group
**Audience signals:** Interest: Fitness, Health, Gym, CrossFit, FIFO/mining
**Retargeting:** Layer in GA4 events (viewed pricing page, clicked join)

## UTM Construction (Google Ads)

```
utm_source=google
utm_medium=paid_search
utm_campaign=[objective]-[location]-[month]-[year]
utm_content=[ad-type]-[theme]-[audience]
utm_term={keyword}
```

**Example:**
```
https://chasingbetter247.com.au/join?utm_source=google&utm_medium=paid_search&utm_campaign=membership-malaga-may-2026&utm_content=rsa-fifo&utm_term={keyword}
```

**Note:** Use `{keyword}` dynamic insertion in Google Ads — do NOT hardcode keywords in UTM term. Let Google Ads inject the actual search term.

## Quality Score Optimization Tips

| Factor | What to Do |
|--------|--------|
| CTR | Write compelling headlines — test price vs facility hooks |
| Ad relevance | Keep keyword themes tight — don't mix FIFO + family in same ad group |
| Landing page | Ensure landing page matches the ad promise (don't send sauna searchers to homepage) |
| Ad extensions | Enable ALL extensions — they improve Quality Score |
| Negative keywords | Add: "personal trainer", "online", "app" at campaign level to avoid irrelevant clicks |

**Negative keyword list (add to all campaigns):**
```
- personal trainer (we have PT but these are informational)
- gym membership cancel
- gym jobs
- gym near me "closed" / "not open"
- free gym
- cheapest gym (too low intent)
```
