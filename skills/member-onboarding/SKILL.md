## Member Journey Stages

```
PROSPECT → VISITOR → TRIAL → MEMBER → ACTIVE MEMBER → RETAINED
    ↓          ↓         ↓        ↓           ↓            ↓
  [AD/NTA]  [1st Visit] [Free trial] [Signup] [30 days]  [90+ days]
```

---

## Stage 1: Prospect (Pre-Visit)

### Touchpoints
- Ad click → Landing page → Form fill
- Welcome email (immediate)

### Goals
- Confirm their interest
- Set expectations for first visit
- Reduce anxiety ("what's it like?")

### Key Messages
- "We're excited to have you"
- "Here's what to expect on your first visit"
- "No ego, no judgment, just results"

---

## Stage 2: First Visitor

### Touchpoints
- In-person greeting at front desk
- First visit email (sent 2 hours after visit)

### Goals
- Make them feel welcome
- Book them for a facility tour or PT session
- Capture their ICP (what brought them in)

### Key Messages
- "Welcome to the CB247 family"
- "Here's what makes us different"
- "Your journey starts now"

---

## Stage 3: Trial Member (Free Trial Period)

### Touchpoints
- Trial confirmation email (Day 0)
- Check-in email (Day 3)
- Offer email (Day 5-7)
- SMS follow-up (Day 7)

### Goals
- Drive second visit
- Introduce them to different areas (recovery, classes)
- Capture member feedback

### Key Messages
- "How was your first week?"
- "Have you tried our [recovery / classes] yet?"
- "Members who visit 3x in week 1 stay for 6+ months"

---

## Stage 4: New Member (First 30 Days)

### Touchpoints
- Welcome packet (Day 1)
- Day 3: "Getting started guide"
- Day 7: "Your first week" check-in
- Day 14: "Building the habit"
- Day 21: "Community connection"
- Day 30: "You're official" milestone

### Goals
- Help them build workout habits
- Connect them with their ICP community (FIFO, families, etc.)
- Introduce them to premium services (PT, classes)

### Key Messages
- "Consistency beats intensity"
- "The best time to start was yesterday. The second best is today."
- "You've got [X] gym buddies here who understand your schedule"

---

## Stage 5: Active Member (30-90 Days)

### Touchpoints
- Monthly check-in email
- Class spotlight (based on interests)
- Referral invitation (once they're engaged)
- Retention offer for at-risk members

### Goals
- Maintain engagement frequency
- Introduce them to all facility areas
- Create community belonging

### Key Messages
- "Variety keeps it interesting"
- "Your [FIFO / family / recovery] community is here"
- "Bring a mate — they get a free week"

---

## Stage 6: Retained Member (90+ Days)

### Touchpoints
- Quarterly milestone celebration
- Referral program invitation
- Re-engagement for low-visit members
- Loyalty rewards (anniversary, streak badges)

### Goals
- Prevent churn
- Maximize lifetime value
- Turn members into advocates

### Key Messages
- "You're officially part of the 8,000+ CB247 family"
- "Share the love — give a friend a free week"
- "Thank you for choosing us"

## Onboarding Sequence Templates

### New Member Email Sequence (Post-Signup)

| Day | Subject | Goal | CTA |
|-----|---------|------|-----|
| 0 | "Welcome to CB247 — Let's get you started" | Confirm signup, set expectations | Book facility tour |

## Onboarding Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| First visit rate | % of form fills who visit | >60% |
| Second visit rate | % of first visitors who return | >40% |
| Trial conversion | % of trials who become members | >30% |
| 30-day retention | % of members active at 30 days | >80% |
| 90-day retention | % of members active at 90 days | >65% |
| Net Promoter Score | "How likely to recommend?" | >50 |
| Referral rate | % of members who refer | Growing |

## Onboarding Audit Checklist

### For Every New Member
- [ ] Welcome email sent within 1 hour
- [ ] ICP captured (FIFO / Family / etc.)
- [ ] Front desk greeted them by name
- [ ] They received facility tour
- [ ] First follow-up scheduled within 48 hours
- [ ] Any special needs noted (injuries, goals)

### At 30 Days
- [ ] Member has visited at least 3 times
- [ ] Member introduced to relevant areas (recovery / classes)
- [ ] Member connected to community (Facebook group, etc.)
- [ ] Member feedback collected
