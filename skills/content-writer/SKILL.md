# SKILL: Content Writer — ChasingBetter247

## Trigger
Auto-activate when task involves: caption, post, copy, content,
reel script, ad copy, email, blog, SMS

## Rules
- Always read context/brand-voice.md before writing
- Always read context/strategy-pestle-swot.md for positioning
- Max caption length: 150 words for Instagram
- First line must be a hook — no fluff openers
- End with ONE clear CTA only

## Content Formulas

### Instagram Reel Script
Line 1 (Hook):    Bold statement or question — max 8 words
Line 2 (Problem): Pain point the viewer feels
Line 3 (Solution): How CB247 solves it
Line 4 (Proof):   Specific detail (price, facility, member count)
CTA:              One action, direct

### Instagram Caption
Hook (1 line) → Context (2-3 lines) → CTA (1 line)
Hashtags: 5-8 max, relevant only

### Facebook Post
Conversational opener → Story or benefit → Community angle → CTA
Longer form OK — Perth locals respond to local references

### Ad Copy (Meta)
Headline:    Max 40 chars — benefit-led
Description: Max 125 chars — price anchor + differentiator
CTA Button:  "Learn More" or "Sign Up"

## Competitor Positioning in Copy
vs Revo:      "Same price, but we have [specific facility]"
vs Ryderwear: "Train hard AND [family/variety benefit]"
vs Anytime:   "Local gym, premium facilities, half the price"
General:      Never name competitors directly in public content

## FIFO-Specific Content
- Lead with: flexibility, no-fuss, freeze option
- Tone: respect their lifestyle, don't over-explain
- Best format: short punchy Reels or Facebook posts
