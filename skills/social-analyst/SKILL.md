## Social Audit Framework

### Internal Audit — @chasingbetter247

#### Metrics to Extract
| Metric | Definition | Good | Target |
|--------|-----------|------|--------|
| Engagement Rate | (likes+comments+saves)/reach × 100 | >3% | >3% |
| Reach Rate | reach/followers × 100 | >15% | >15% |
| Save Rate | saves/reach × 100 | >2% | >2% |
| Comment Rate | comments/reach × 100 | >0.5% | >0.5% |
| Share Rate | shares/reach × 100 | >0.3% | >0.3% |
| Follower Growth | new followers/week | Growing | >2%/week |
| Video Completion | watch time/views × 100 | >45% | >45% for Reels |

#### Best Performing Content Analysis
For top 5 posts in last 30 days, analyze:
- **Hook:** What stopped the scroll?
- **Format:** Reel vs Carousel vs Static
- **Topic:** What ICP did it speak to?
- **Length:** How long was caption?
- **CTA:** What call-to-action used?
- **Timing:** Day of week + time posted
- **Hashtags:** Which set drove reach?

#### Posting Time Analysis
Map engagement by:
- Day of week (Mon-Sun)
- Time of day (6am / 8am / 12pm / 5pm / 8pm AWST)
- Identify top 3 performing windows
