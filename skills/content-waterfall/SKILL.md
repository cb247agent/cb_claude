## The Waterfall Method

One core idea → Multiple platform-ready assets

### Efficiency Ratio
| Input | Output |
|-------|--------|
| 1 Core Idea | 1 long-form IG caption + 1 IG Reel script + 3 Reel captions + 5 stories + 1 FB post + 2 ad variants + 1 email + 1 SMS |

### Platform Distribution

```
CORE IDEA (1)
     │
     ├── Instagram Feed (1)
     │      ├── Caption (1)
     │      └── Hashtag Set (1)
     │
     ├── Instagram Reels (3)
     │      ├── Reel A Hook (3-8s)
     │      ├── Reel B Hook (3-8s)
     │      └── Reel C Hook (3-8s)
     │
     ├── Instagram Stories (5)
     │      ├── Story 1 (Hook)
     │      ├── Story 2 (Context)
     │      ├── Story 3 (Detail)
     │      ├── Story 4 (CTA)
     │      └── Story 5 (Proof)
     │
     ├── Facebook (1)
     │      └── Long-form post (1)
     │
     ├── Meta Ad Variants (2)
     │      ├── Ad Variant A
     │      └── Ad Variant B
     │
     ├── Email (1)
     │      └── Promotional email
     │
     └── SMS (1)
            └── Short message

TOTAL: 14-16 assets from 1 idea
```

## Platform-Specific Templates

### Instagram Feed Caption
```
[HOOK — 1-2 lines that stop the scroll]

[BODY — 3-5 lines expanding on the idea.
Keep it conversational. Speak to [FIFO worker / family / newcomer].
Include one specific proof point or number.
End with a relatable observation.]

[CTA — 1 clear action]

#[hashtag1] #[hashtag2] #[hashtag3] #[hashtag4] #[hashtag5]
```

### Instagram Reel Script (3 variants)

**Variant A — The Story Hook**
```
HOOK (0-3s): [Personal story opener]
PROBLEM (3-8s): [The frustration FIFO workers feel]
SOLUTION (8-20s): [How CB247 solves it]
PROOF (20-30s): [Specific detail or statistic]
CTA (30s): [One action, direct]
```

**Variant B — The Contrast Hook**
```
HOOK (0-3s): [Before/after contrast]
PROBLEM (3-8s): [What other gyms lack]
SOLUTION (8-20s): [What CB247 has instead]
PROOF (20-30s): [Specific feature or member moment]
CTA (30s): [One action, direct]
```

**Variant C — The Stat Hook**
```
HOOK (0-3s): [Surprising statistic]
PROBLEM (3-8s): [The underlying pain point]
SOLUTION (8-20s): [Why CB247 members don't feel it]
PROOF (20-30s): [Member benefit or feature]
CTA (30s): [One action, direct]
```
